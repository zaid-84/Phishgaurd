About PhishGuard

Learn how we help protect you from online threats

Our Mission

At PhishGuard, we're committed to making the internet a safer place for everyone. Our mission is to provide accessible tools that help users identify and avoid phishing attacks, protecting personal data and preventing financial fraud.

How It Works

Our phishing detection tool uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze URLs for common characteristics of phishing websites. The system examines multiple factors including:

  • Domain age and registration information
  • URL structure and redirects
  • SSL certificate validity
  • Website content analysis
  • Comparison with known phishing databases

When you submit a URL, our system processes these factors and provides you with a risk assessment in seconds.

Our Team

PhishGuard was developed by a team of cybersecurity experts and data scientists passionate about online safety. our team continuously updates our detection algorithms to stay ahead of new phishing techniques.
